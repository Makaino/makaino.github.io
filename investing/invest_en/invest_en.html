Become a Girls Best Friends' investor: strong product, advanced technology and good terms.

Best Girls Friends is an augmented reality mobile application. We use information technology to bring customers closer to jewelry. We offer you to become our investor at an early stage of a product development.

Strong product.

People love jewelry. This is clear and close to anyone. However, there are many barriers between a piece of jewelry and a consumer. You have to go to a shopping center, try on jewelry that was tried on by someone else before, or perform an online search with a magnifying glass. We have created a system that erases all these obstacles. Instead of guessing “Will this ring suit me…” we rely on augmented reality technologies.

Hi-tech

At the heart of our application there is an augmented reality technology. It is already being used by such giants as IKEA, WANNAKICKS and other industry flagships.

Good terms.
