Erasing boundaries between you and your perfect piece of jewelry.

'Girls Best Friends' is a mobile app in a format of augmented reality which helps you try on jewelry in any convenient place: at home, during lunchtime, at work, or even while standing in line for another jewelry.

How does it work?

Just like a mask photo on Instagram or Snapchat. What you'll need is a smartphone with our application and a working camera. In the application, select the jewelry you like, for example, earrings. Point the camera and - voila - a piece of jewelry flaunts on your ear. You are gorgeous!

Works with any kind of products: rings, earrings, necklaces, and even designer jewelry of complex shapes. Integrates with online stores, CRM systems and social media networks.

Together with Girls Best Friends

Skip the queues

For those who like to try on the entire range of jewelry before buying. There are those who like to try on one, two... fifteen jewelry items. Already after the fourth fitting, even a well-mannered salesperson will become impatient, especially during the days of promotions and sales. They are more interested in those who are ready to buy right now, which is fine, but inconvenient. With our application, at a comfortable pace one can try even the entire store range on, and find a perfect piece of jewelry.

Don`t bother

Appreciate the shades of precious stones and metal shine without leaving your home. Jewelry is like underwear - you won't understand if it fits you, until you try it on. Our users try on the jewelry they like in any place comfortable for them. You no longer need to drive to a mall, wasting time on the road, or searching through an online store with a magnifying glass.

Take care of yourself

Do not try on items that have been tried on by others. A beautiful ring in the store has most likely been tried on by a hundred women before. Some of them might have not sanitized their hands properly. With our application, you don't need to try on after others. There is only you, your personal smartphone and the jewelry you like.

Feel comfortable

No one will rate you on your purchasing power. The buyer is alone with the jewelry. You are free to try on either Tiffany & Co exclusives, or SUNLIGHT sale . Post your looks with jewelry on Instagram, collect likes and opinions, ask your friends what suited you best. The user is the master of the situation and decides what to choose.
